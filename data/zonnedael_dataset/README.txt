Explanation of datasets “Zonnedael” collected in the Netherlands

This dataset contains actual consumption data from approximately 80 addresses, with time intervals of 15 minutes for electricity and 60 minutes for gas.

The data comes from smart meters, for which users gave consent to read out the measurement data from the smart meter for research purposes.

The data units are Wh per 15 minutes for electricity and m³ per hour for gas.
Where data was missing or unrealistic, it has been corrected.

As a result, it may occur that in certain periods, the actual usage is not shown, but rather an average value is displayed.

Some consumption series have been removed because they were incomplete and/or contained unrealistic values.

Electricity and gas data can be linked to household type, type of dwelling, and age of the dwelling, per connection, via the anonymous name 'Customer #' and through the file "Klanttypering" (Customer Typing).

The measurement period for electricity runs from 01-05-2012 to 07-03-2014, and for gas from 01-05-2012 to 07-01-2014.

The time indication refers to local Dutch time, including daylight saving time (summer/winter time).
